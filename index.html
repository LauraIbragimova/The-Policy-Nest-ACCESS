ACCESS Rollout Plan – Prototype Output

This static example illustrates how the ACCESS rollout plan will be structured. Later versions will generate and update this content automatically from ACCESS policy requirements.

Phase 1 – Readiness Assessment

  • Task: Confirm ACCESS eligibility and condition track selection
    Owner: VBC / Strategy Lead
  • Task: Designate ACCESS Clinical Director and document responsibilities
    Owner: Chief Medical Officer
  • Task: Review existing technology and reporting capabilities against ACCESS requirements
    Owner: IT / Analytics Lead

Phase 2 – Operational Design

  • Task: Map current care workflows to ACCESS care model for selected condition track(s)
    Owner: Clinical Operations
  • Task: Define care team roles (care coordinator, tech liaison, clinical lead)
    Owner: Operations / HR
  • Task: Draft patient alignment and enrollment process, including handling of control group assignments
    Owner: Care Coordination
